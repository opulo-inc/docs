## Why homing is important

Homing establishes a known starting position for the machine.

During homing, the LumenPnP moves the head to its X, Y, and Z zero position, located at the `Front-left corner (X0, Y0)` of the machine.

---

## Before Homing

**Before pressing the Home button**, confirm the nozzles are level.

<div class="stop-if"> <div class="stop-if-title"> Stop If </div>

Stop if both nozzles are not close to level. They do not have to be perfectly level.

<div> Homing while the nozzles are misaligned can cause the nozzle to collide with the machine frame or staging plate. </div>

Adjust the nozzles so they are level before continuing.

</div>

<div class="pro-tip"> <div class="pro-tip-title">
Pro Tip
</div>

Using the Z Park button guarantees that the nozzle tip moves to a safe height.

<div> It is encouraged to get into the habbit of doing this frequently while learning the controls. </div>

<div> Keeping the nozzle at a safe height greatly reduces the chance of accidental crashes. </div>

The Park button is found in Machine Controls between the Z height adjustment arrows

</div>

---

## Perform the Homing Operation

1. Locate the `Home button` in the `Machine Controls` panel.

<img src="images/home-machine-controls.webp" width="40%">

1. Click the `yellow Home icon`.
    * The machine will move the head to the `Front-left corner (X0, Y0)` of the machine.
    * Once the movement completes, the head will remain parked in this position.

<img src="../../../v4/calibration/2-connect-to-machine/images/11-home-your-machine-gif.gif" width="60%">
